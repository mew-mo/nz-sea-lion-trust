About NZ Sea Lions

A population surviving
New Zealand Sea Lions are critically endangered, and one of the rarest sea lion species in the world.

Threat status: Endemic species, main breeding colony in decline


Found in: Most of the population at Auckland and Campbell subantarctic islands. Emerging breeding locations at Stewart Island/Rakiura, Otago and Southland regions


Threats: Diseases, fisheries interactions, pup mortality, food availability, human impacts
